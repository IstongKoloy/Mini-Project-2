GSIX Parking Management System - Features



User registration

Users should be able to register on the website and create a profile.

Parking usage tracking

Users should be able to track their parking usage and view their current balance.

Parking regulations

The website should clearly outline the regulations and policies of the parking management system.

Access Control System

Integrate parking passes with access control system - boom barriers, biometrics, etc.

Auto-Generated Passes

QR code-based passes are instantly auto-generated for a visitor.

Customize Spaces

Customize & reserve parking space as per company, staff, pay-&-park, etc.

On-Spot Or Monthly Pay

Parking passes generated can be on pay-&-park or a monthly pay basis. It is FASTag enabled.

Slots Per Vehicle Type

Allocate slots as per two-wheeler and four-wheeler vehicles to utilise all available space.

View Live Reports

No more manual checking of empty space. View it on your phone. Also view live reports.

Additional Charge Free

No additional charge for accepting credit card payments (over 70 online payment gateways are supported)

Mail Update

Automated mailing to clients (before / after stay, after reserving etc.)

Administrative functions

The website should have a section for administrative tasks such as managing parking spots, rules, and rates.
